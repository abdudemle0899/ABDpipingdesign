About CRTEC

Specialized Engineering & 3D Plant Design Excellence

CRTEC Industrial Piping Design Team is a specialized engineering and 3D plant design department based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, delivering comprehensive engineering solutions for the chemical, petrochemical, pharmaceutical, fertilizer, energy, and industrial manufacturing sectors.

In collaboration with Hebei Yingke Petrochemical Engineering Co., Ltd., we provide end-to-end engineering support ranging from process-oriented piping design and detailed 3D plant modeling to engineering documentation and project execution support.

Core Services

Comprehensive Engineering Solutions

From 3D plant modeling to piping stress analysis, we deliver end-to-end engineering services for industrial projects worldwide.

🏭

3D Plant Modeling & Digital Engineering

Complete plant, workshop, structural steel, equipment, walls, floors, and roof modeling using AVEVA PDMS/E3D, including plant digitization and Digital Twin development.

🔧

Piping Engineering & Stress Analysis

3D pipe routing based on P&IDs, piping layout optimization, pipe support design, flexibility studies, and third-party piping stress analysis using CAESAR II.

📋

Engineering Documentation

Preparation of isometric drawings, pipe layouts, general arrangement (GA) drawings, Material Take-Offs (MTO), anti-corrosion and insulation schedules using AutoCAD.

⛽

Fuel Terminal & Tank Farm Engineering

Engineering design, layout development, piping systems, storage facilities, and associated infrastructure for fuel terminals and tank farms.

🔄

Plant Modification & Brownfield Engineering

Debottlenecking, revamp studies, brownfield modifications, expansion projects, and fit-for-service evaluations of existing industrial facilities.

📊

Tender Evaluation & Technical Consultancy

Independent technical tender evaluation, design review, scope verification, contract assessment, and owners engineering services for petrochemical projects.

🤝

EPC & Project Support Services

Engineering support for EPC contractors including design review, procurement support, installation oversight, commissioning, and turnkey project execution.

👥

Engineering Outsourcing & Resource Augmentation

Provision of dedicated engineering teams and specialized design resources for international engineering companies, consultants, and industrial project developers.
