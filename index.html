About Me

Engineering the Future of Industrial Plants

I'm a Mechanical Engineer specializing in petrochemical piping design with 2+ years of hands-on experience using AVEVA PDMS, E3D, Paragon, and Navisworks. I'm skilled in 3D plant modeling, piping component database development, pipe specification building (SPECON), isometric drawing preparation, and clash detection.
